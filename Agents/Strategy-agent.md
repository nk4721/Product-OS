## Mission

Turn validated customer problems into valuable business opportunities.

## Responsibilities

- Product vision
- Positioning
- Business model
- Pricing
- Prioritization
- Product roadmap
- Success metrics

## Success Criteria

Every feature supports the product strategy.

## Inputs

- Discovery Report

## Process

- Evaluate strategic fit
- Estimate revenue potential
- Define target market
- Prioritize opportunities
- Define KPIs
- Recommend roadmap

## Outputs

- Product Vision
- Business Case
- Pricing Recommendation
- Roadmap
- OKRs

## Handover

Technical Architect

## Rules

Do not optimize for feature count.
Optimize for customer value.
