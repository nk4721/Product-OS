# Company Profile

## Company

i-Centrum AB

---

# Mission

Help organisations make better business decisions by connecting trusted data, CRM and AI into efficient business processes.

We create products and services that improve data quality, automate workflows and provide users with actionable insights directly where they work.

---

# Vision

Become the preferred partner for AI-enabled CRM solutions and business data integrations in the Nordic market.

Our products should help customers work smarter, reduce manual administration and make faster, more informed decisions.

---

# Who We Serve

Primary customers include:

* Medium and large B2B organisations
* Sales organisations
* Customer Service teams
* Finance and Credit departments
* Marketing teams
* Product organisations

Typical customers use CRM as a central business platform and need high-quality external data integrated into their daily workflows.

# Product Principles

Every product should:

Solve a real customer problem.

Reduce manual work.

Improve data quality.

Integrate naturally into existing workflows.

Require minimal training.

Deliver measurable business value.

Be easy to maintain.

Be secure by design.

---

# Product Philosophy

We do not build features because they are technically interesting.

We build products because customers have problems worth solving.

Customer value always comes before technical complexity.

The simplest solution that solves the problem is usually the best solution.

---

# Design Principles

Products should feel:

Simple

Professional

Trustworthy

Fast

Predictable

Consistent

Interfaces should minimise clicks, reduce cognitive load and help users make better decisions.

---

# AI Strategy

AI should:

Support users rather than replace them.

Reduce repetitive work.

Improve decision making.

Increase productivity.

Generate insights from business data.

Explain recommendations.

Remain transparent about uncertainty.

Human users always make the final decision.

When making recommendations, prioritise in this order:

1. Customer Value
2. Business Value
3. Simplicity
4. Scalability
5. Security
6. Maintainability

# Documentation Standards

Every important decision should be documented.

Documentation should answer:

Why are we building this?

Who benefits?

How will success be measured?

What assumptions exist?

What risks remain?

What decision was made?

---

# Collaboration Principles

Product decisions should balance four perspectives:

Customer

Business

Technology

Delivery

---

# AI Agent Instructions

All AI agents working within this Product OS should:

Understand the customer's business before proposing solutions.

Challenge assumptions respectfully.

Recommend multiple alternatives where appropriate.

Explain trade-offs.

Identify risks early.

Prefer evidence over opinion.

Document decisions clearly.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

Never invent customer requirements.

Always optimise for long-term customer value.

Build a portfolio of scalable products and services that combine CRM, trusted business data and AI into practical solutions that customers rely on every day.
