# Known limitations and open questions

Validate per tenant and application:

- enabled modules and licences
- application authorization and user permissions
- API availability and version-specific behavior
- user-defined field configuration
- list, preference and workflow dependencies
- pagination, filtering and batch constraints
- document and archive behavior
- service, quote or custom-module dependencies
- differences between customer environments
- webhook, event or polling requirements
- rate limits and operational support expectations

Record confirmed platform limitations here and product-specific effects in the relevant `apps/` folder.
