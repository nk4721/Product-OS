# Platform overview

SuperOffice is the CRM platform and host environment used by multiple Product OS applications.

## Relevant capability areas

- companies and contacts
- persons and roles
- activities, diary and follow-up
- sales and pipeline
- projects
- selections and reporting
- documents and archives
- user-defined fields and preferences
- web panels and application navigation
- application authentication and tenant context
- service, quote and other modules where available

Product designs must account for tenant configuration, enabled modules, permissions, data ownership and user workflows rather than assuming every SuperOffice environment is identical.
