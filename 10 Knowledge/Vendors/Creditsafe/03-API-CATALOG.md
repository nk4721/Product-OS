# API capability catalog

Use this as a capability map, not an endpoint specification.

## Capability groups to validate

- company search and matching
- company profile and registered information
- credit assessment and reports
- financial and key company figures
- directors, signatories, ownership or related roles
- company monitoring and change events
- prospecting and company selection
- international company coverage
- person or consumer services where separately licensed

For every product feature, record the exact contracted API, countries, entitlement, identifiers, schema version, storage rights and fallback behavior.
