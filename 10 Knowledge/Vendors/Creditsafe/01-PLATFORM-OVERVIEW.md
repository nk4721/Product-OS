# Platform overview

Creditsafe is treated as an external provider of company, credit, monitoring and related business information services. Exact capabilities, country coverage and permitted use vary by product and customer agreement.

## Product OS use cases

- company search and validation
- CRM enrichment and controlled updates
- credit checks and reports
- monitoring of relevant company changes
- prospecting and segmentation where licensed
- management, ownership and related company information where available

## Boundary

Creditsafe knowledge belongs here. Customer-facing scope, pricing, roadmap and releases belong in `apps/creditsafe-for-superoffice/`.

## Validation required

Confirm contracted products, environments, countries, identifiers, schemas, rate limits, retention rights and support model before committing scope.
