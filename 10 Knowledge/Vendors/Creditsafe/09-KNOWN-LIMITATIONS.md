# Known limitations and open questions

Validate per implementation:

- contracted products and endpoints
- country and entity coverage
- identifiers and matching quality
- report availability and format
- rating, score and limit semantics by country
- monitoring events and delivery guarantees
- rate limits and batch constraints
- storage, display and redistribution rights
- person-data access and retention requirements
- differences between test and production

Record confirmed limitations here and product-specific consequences in the relevant product folder.
