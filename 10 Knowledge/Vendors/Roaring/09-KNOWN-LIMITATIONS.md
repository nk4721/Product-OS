# Known limitations and open questions

Do not assume uniform data coverage, freshness, identifiers, schemas or legal rights across countries and API products.

Validate before each product commitment:

- contracted capabilities and customer entitlements
- supported countries and entity types
- response completeness and update frequency
- rate limits, batch limits and monitoring behaviour
- storage, display, export and retention rights
- sandbox representativeness
- webhook delivery guarantees and replay options
- support and incident escalation model

Record product-specific gaps in the product repository. A limitation is not resolved merely because a technical workaround exists; commercial, privacy and operational consequences must also be approved.
