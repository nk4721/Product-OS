# Security and privacy

Use Roaring data only for an approved purpose and within the applicable customer agreement, licence and legal basis.

## Controls

- Apply least-privilege access to APIs, application functions and stored data.
- Keep credentials in managed secret storage and encrypt data in transit and at rest.
- Minimize personal and compliance data collected, displayed, logged and retained.
- Define retention and deletion rules before production use.
- Record relevant searches, writes and decisions for audit without over-logging personal data.
- Separate customer tenants and supplier-specific data.
- Restrict exports and bulk access.
- Complete privacy, security and contractual review before enabling sensitive workflows.

Never use compliance data as an unexplained automated decision. Human review and traceability are required where outcomes materially affect people or organizations.
