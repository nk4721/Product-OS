# Platform overview

Roaring is treated as an external provider of company, person and compliance-related data services. Exact services, countries and data rights vary by API product and customer agreement.

## Common use cases

- Validate organizations or people during onboarding.
- Enrich CRM records with authoritative attributes.
- Retrieve ownership, management or signatory information.
- Support KYC and AML workflows.
- Monitor entities for relevant changes.
- Reduce manual lookup and duplicate entry.

A Roaring-based product should add workflow value rather than only expose raw data. Confirm contracted APIs, environments, coverage, identifiers, limits, update frequency, storage rights and support before committing scope.
