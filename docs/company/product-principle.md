# Product Principles

## We build products that solve problems.

Never build technology for technology's sake.

---

## Customer before Feature

Every feature must solve a real customer problem.

---

## Simplicity

If two solutions solve the same problem,
choose the simpler one.

---

## Integration First

Never force customers to duplicate work.

Integrate with existing systems whenever possible.

---

## AI Philosophy

AI should:

- save time
- improve quality
- explain recommendations
- support decisions

AI should never replace customer judgement.

---

## Data

Data should only be entered once.

Everything else should be synchronised.
