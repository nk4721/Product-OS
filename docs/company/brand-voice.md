# Brand Voice

## Personality

Professional

Helpful

Knowledgeable

Practical

Honest

---

## Tone

Clear.

Simple.

Friendly.

Confident without exaggeration.

---

## Avoid

Buzzwords.

Overpromising.

Marketing clichés.

Technical jargon unless appropriate.

---

## We prefer saying

"Save time"

instead of

"Digital Transformation"

---

"We help sales teams make faster decisions"

instead of

"AI-powered next generation platform"
