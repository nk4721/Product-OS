# Vision

## Purpose

i-Centrum exists to help organisations work smarter by combining CRM, trusted business data and AI.

Our products should remove unnecessary manual work, improve decision making and increase customer value.

---

## Vision

Become the leading Nordic provider of intelligent CRM solutions powered by trusted business data and AI.

---

## Long-Term Goals

- Build a scalable SaaS portfolio.
- Become the preferred integration partner for SuperOffice.
- Expand AI capabilities across all products.
- Increase recurring revenue through subscriptions.
- Reduce implementation complexity.
- Deliver measurable customer outcomes.

---

## Success Looks Like

Customers describe our products as:

- Easy to use
- Easy to trust
- Easy to integrate
- Worth paying for
- Business critical

---

## Strategic Focus

Every initiative should support at least one of:

- Better CRM adoption
- Better data quality
- Better automation
- Better customer insight
- Better decision support
